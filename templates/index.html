Centralized Platform Empowering Insights and Innovations

TOP Sphere envisioned as a centralized, comprehensive platform that showcases the wide array of initiatives and contributions by the TechOps Excellence Team. It serves as a unified access point, offering users a seamless experience to navigate, explore, and interact with the team’s insights, reports, and products. It simplifies how users and stakeholders access key information, enhancing transparency, visibility, and collaboration. Designed to scale with evolving business needs. Its open structure allows for future expansion into embedding reports from various BI tools

Our Responsibilities

The TechOps Excellence Team plays a vital role in delivering strategic insights to leadership. Our key responsibilities include providing comprehensive Power BI reports that cover critical metrics such as ServiceNow Incident Count, Mean Time to Resolve (MTTR), Mean Time to Detect (MTTD), and service downtime analytics. We also develop innovative solutions like Snowgate and TopVista, while collaborating with platforms such as O&L, CMDB, and APM Terminals. Additionally, we support external audit projects to ensure operational excellence.

Key Benefits

    • Versatile Reporting: By hosting or embedding reports from various BI tools, we reduce future dependencies on specific tools like Power BI.
    • Cost-Effective: A solution that is scalable and economical for the organization.
    • Enhanced User Support: An integrated chatbot to assist users with terminology and help them understand TechOps Excellence processes.
    • Faster Time to Market: All team deliverables are hosted on TOP Sphere, ensuring they are always available online.
    • Cross-Team Collaboration: This shared platform allows other teams to learn about the work being done by TechOps Excellence, fostering greater transparency and collaboration.

Features

    • Centralized Repository: By hosting or embedding reports from various BI tools, we reduce future dependencies on specific tools like Power BI.
    • Product Showcase: Easy access to the team’s successfully launched products.
    • Comprehensive Documentation: Navigation to extensive Confluence documentation.
    • User Feedback: A feedback form is available for users to help improve and refine our work.

    Goal: We aim to engage with teams across the organization to demonstrate how our work can be of value, fostering greater collaboration and utility.
